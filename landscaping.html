Landscaping services

What is Landscaping?

Landscaping is the process of increasing curb appeal of a home or business through additional features that include but are not limited to plants, trees, shrubs, hardscape, flowers, pine straw, mulch, sod, etc.

Pine straw installation

Pine straw installation is the process of adding high quality pine needles around plants to increase curb appeal.

Plants/Flowers

Kelly Landscapes supports local nursery’s through buying the best plants and flowers for customers homes and businesses.

Retaining walls

Retaining walls are often used to hold dirt or water in an area. Retaining walls add to the curb appeal of a home and can provide a unique area to plant flowers, shrubs, etc.

SOD installation

Kelly Landscapes uses high quality, top notch sod for customers. Sod is pieces of grass ready to be placed on grated, level, and prepared ground.

Walkway/Steps

Walkways are a great way to add curb appeal to a home. Kelly Landscapes can add walkways, and multiple other hardscapes to help you enjoy your yard.

Water/Lighting features

We offer a wide variety of water and lighting features to make your home the best on the block. Request a quote to see the options we offer.
