About us

Kelly Landscapes Management, Inc. was established in 1992 starting out strictly as a maintenance company and has evolved into a full-service company offering lawn maintenance, irrigation, landscape design and installation, lawn care, hardscapes, outdoor living spaces, and property sweeping. Kelly Landscapes Management is a proven leader in the green industry servicing the metro Atlanta area. Our experience includes both commercial and residential properties, including athletic fields, schools and government complexes. Kelly Landscapes Management prides itself in quality work and customer service.
